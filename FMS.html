Researches on
current system

Three major problems I found based on interviewing 5 RIT students about current school's facility reporting system:


  • Familiarity: student don't know where to report facility issues.
  • Complexity: current online reporting form is time-consuming and not user-friendly;
  • Reliability: students prefer to contact with people instead of submitting online forms.

Technologies Relied On

Computerized maintenance management (CMV)


Allow requesters to submit work order requests and system to assign tasks to managers.

Computer vision
(CV)


Students can submit requests with photos and CV system can help to identify problems.

Natural language processing (NLP)


Students can manually describe problems and system can format descriptions into classified job types.

Design for motivation

Encourage students to use this reporting system from 3 aspects:


  • Promote: set multiple download points; host on other widely-used campus app; flyers and posters; rewards for reporting;
  • Simplify: clean interface; less client-side input; automatic content filling;
  • Connect: quick feedback; real-time updates; information safety; progress status checking.
