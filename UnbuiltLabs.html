My role & goals

As a product design intern at Unbuilt Labs, a think tank - media company hybrid providing strategic foresight & sustainability initiatives. I am responsible for researching and bringing a new editorial experience to the company's online products.

Two main goals in this project are:

  • Designing a research feed page under its business website to showcase its teams' research projects.
  • Building style guide for unifying its entire online presence, internal communication and marketing purpose;

Design constraints

Considering articles on research feed page should be easily transferred to company's website homepage.

Two main constraints are:

  • Each article preview on research feed page should be a repeatable component within a grid system;
  • Research feed should be differentiated from the editorial feed but maintaining the same image ratio and content elements in each component.

Layout decision

I chose to go with Grid layout 2 because:


  • Each article preview has an even screen space to gain attention from audiences;
  • Clear boarder between each grid component to decrease ambiguity.
  • A good implementation affordability.
