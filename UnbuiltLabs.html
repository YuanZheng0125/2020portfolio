My role & goals

As a product design intern at Unbuilt Labs, I am responsible for researching and bringing a new editorial experience to the company's online products.

Two main goals in this project are:

  • Building style guide for unifying its entire online presence, internal communication and marketing purpose;
  • Designing a research feed page under its business website to showcase its teams' research projects.

Brand Style Guide

To facilitate company's brand campaign, I create a style guide for company's marketing and communication needs.
Scroll down to see an interactive style guide prototypes.

Design constraints on research feed

Considering articles on research feed page should be easily transferred to company's website homepage.

Two main constraints are:

  • Each article preview on research feed page should be a repeatable component within a grid system;
  • Research feed should be differentiated from the editorial feed but maintaining the same image ratio and content elements in each component.

Design approach

My plan is using a web-first strategy to define the information architecture of the research feed page at first.
Using web-first approach instead of mobile-first web design because I realized that the grid system is the key point I need to tackle with, on which web design has more challenges.

Thinking Process

Inputs from audiences

A survey was conducted by me to understand user needs for an editorial website experience. Results below are summarized based on 5 responses:

  • Users like stylish, modern-look design keeping them away from being bored.
  • Things compel them to click an article include article title, tagline and image preview.
Competitive Analysis

Decision on grid

  • 5-column grid was chosen as a start point because it brings the best variations on each grid layout but also maintains enough space for large image preview.
  • No margin around each grid component to create a modern aesthetic.

Layout decision

I chose to go with Grid layout 2 because:


  • Each article preview has an even screen space to gain attention from audiences;
  • Clear boarder between each grid component to decrease ambiguity.
  • A good implementation affordability.

Adding an introduction banner

Considering it has a great chance that users enter this page through search engines or social media, I designed a banner to make the page more self-speaking.
The outline stroked font is inherited from an uniform header style across Unbuilt Labs website other pages.

Wrap-up

As a design intern at a startup, I got a valuable chance to build the company's brand style guide. I was able to take a close look at how it helps to form my outputs as well as the company's marketing campaign.
I was also impressed by how design constraints can fasten my work through giving me a focus to deal with. Like in this project, while I had constraints on image ratio and visual style, it actually led me to understand that playing with grids is the way I should go with.
